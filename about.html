Mega Region 2

Our Region in North America

MFM Mega Region 2 is a regional jurisdiction of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries in the United States of America. Headquartered at MFM USA Prayer City in Houston, Texas, the region oversees multiple branches and serves as a center for prayer, deliverance, and the advancement of the gospel across North America.

Under the leadership of Pastor Olumide Oni as Principal Regional Overseer, the region operates weekly prayer programs that connect believers across international time zones — including the Healing & Deliverance Hour every Tuesday and the Open Heaven Encounter every Thursday, reaching the USA, Ghana, UK, Nigeria, and Canada.

The monthly Power Must Change Hands (PMCH) programme, held on the first Saturday of every month at Prayer City, remains a cornerstone of the MFM prayer mandate — a gathering where stubborn situations are addressed by fire.

Principal Regional Overseer

Pastor Olumide Oni

Mega Region 2 USA & MFM North America HQ

Pastor Olumide Oni is a prophet, evangelist, pastor, teacher, husband and father — a man through whom the fire of God burns brightly across nations.

His ministry journey within MFM has taken him through multiple assignments of increasing responsibility: from Kubwa to MFM Lugbe Region in Abuja, then to MFM Wuye/Utako (the International Headquarters Annex in Abuja), and now to MFM North America Headquarters at Prayer City, Houston, Texas — where he serves as the Principal Regional Overseer of Mega Region 2.

Known for dynamic teaching, administrative excellence, and a powerful personal testimony of God's deliverance, Pastor Oni leads Mega Region 2 as a vibrant centre of prayer and spiritual warfare, with weekly programmes connecting believers across multiple continents.
