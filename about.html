Mission Objectives

The Mandate of MFM

Eleven pillars that define the purpose and direction of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries worldwide.

To propagate the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ all over the world

To promote the revival of Apostolic signs, wonders and miracles

To bring together children of God who are lost in dead churches

To train believers in the art and science of spiritual warfare

To train believers to receive Holy Ghost baptism and fire

To turn the joy of our enemies to sorrow — Deliverance ministry

To build an aggressive end-time army for the Lord

To deliver those who have become slaves to pastors, prophets and apostles

To build up heavenly-bound and aggressive Christians

To build up prayer eagles

To purify the Pentecostal dirtiness of this age

Principal Regional Overseer

Pastor Olumide Oni

Mega Region 2 USA & MFM North America HQ

Pastor Olumide Oni is a prophet, evangelist, pastor, teacher, husband and father — a man through whom the fire of God burns brightly across nations.

His ministry journey within MFM has taken him through multiple assignments of increasing responsibility: from Kubwa to MFM Lugbe Region in Abuja, then to MFM Wuye/Utako (the International Headquarters Annex in Abuja), and now to MFM North America Headquarters at Prayer City, Houston, Texas — where he serves as the Principal Regional Overseer of Mega Region 2.

Known for dynamic teaching, administrative excellence, and a powerful personal testimony of God's deliverance, Pastor Oni leads Mega Region 2 as a vibrant centre of prayer and spiritual warfare, with weekly programmes connecting believers across multiple continents.

Partner in Ministry

Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni

Regional Coordinator, Women Foundation · Mega Region 2 USA

Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni stands alongside Pastor Olumide Oni as a devoted partner in ministry, faith, and family. Together they serve the congregation of Mega Region 2 with dedication, love, and the fire of the Holy Ghost.

A woman of prayer and purpose, she plays a vital role in the spiritual life and growth of the region, exemplifying the call of Proverbs 31 — strength, dignity, and wisdom in the house of God. As Regional Coordinator of the MFM Women Foundation, she leads with compassion, equipping women for their God-ordained destiny.
