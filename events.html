International Prayer Service

Open Heaven Encounter

Connecting believers across USA, Ghana, UK, Nigeria, and Canada for a powerful weekly encounter with God.

International Prayer · Open Heavens · Encounter

The Open Heaven Encounter is a weekly international prayer service that bridges time zones and continents. Believers from the USA, Ghana, the United Kingdom, Nigeria, and Canada come together under one anointing, pressing into God for open heavens and divine encounters.

  • 🇺🇸 6:00 PM CST (USA)
  • 🇬🇭 12:00 AM GMT (Ghana)
  • 🇬🇧 12:00 AM GMT (United Kingdom)
  • 🇳🇬 1:00 AM WAT (Nigeria)
  • 🇨🇦 7:00 PM EST (Canada)
