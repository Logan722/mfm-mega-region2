Regional Coordinator

Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni

MFM Women Foundation · Mega Region 2 USA

Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni serves faithfully alongside her husband, Pastor Olumide Oni, the Principal Regional Overseer of MFM Mega Region 2 in North America. A woman of deep prayer, unwavering faith, and tender compassion, she carries a burden for the spiritual growth and empowerment of women across the region.

Under her leadership, the Women Foundation equips women with the Word of God, builds a culture of fervent prayer, and provides a space for healing, deliverance, and restoration. Her ministry reflects the heart of a shepherd — nurturing, correcting, and lifting women into their God-ordained destiny.

She is a devoted mother and a living testimony of grace, purpose, and the fire of God at work in the lives of women who dare to believe.

"She is clothed with strength and dignity; she can laugh at the days to come. She speaks with wisdom, and faithful instruction is on her tongue." — Proverbs 31:25–26 (NIV)

Our Focus

Three Pillars of the Women Foundation

Building women of fire, faith, and purpose through the power of the Holy Ghost.

Monthly Meetings

Dedicated gatherings where women come together for Bible study, prayer, and fellowship — strengthening bonds of sisterhood and deepening their walk with God.

Empowerment Programs

Equipping women for excellence in every sphere — career, marriage, motherhood, and ministry — through conferences, workshops, and Spirit-led mentorship.

Deliverance for Women

Targeted prayer sessions and deliverance services addressing the unique spiritual battles women face — breaking chains and releasing divine freedom.
