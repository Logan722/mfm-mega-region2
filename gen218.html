About the Ministry

What is Gen218?

Gen218 — named after Genesis 2:18 — is the MFM Christian Singles Ministry, a divine movement burdened with a vision of preparing singles for marriage. The program addresses singles on every plane — spiritual, financial, psychological, and emotional — until each individual is fully furnished and prepared for God's purpose in marriage.

Through monthly meetings, powerful teachings from the Word of God, prayer sessions, seminars, and fellowship activities, Gen218 creates a supportive and uplifting community for Christian singles seeking meaningful relationships, spiritual growth, and a deeper walk with Christ.

At MFM Mega Region 2, Gen218 is not just a program — it is a family where singles are equipped, empowered, and positioned for destiny. Whether you are newly entering adulthood, navigating the journey of singleness, or a single parent building a future, there is a place for you.

Gen218 Focus Areas

  • Spiritual growth & prayer life
  • Relationship readiness
  • Financial empowerment
  • Emotional & psychological health
  • Career development
  • Marriage preparation

Who Is Gen218 For?

Singles Categories

Gen218 has tailored programs and support for every category of single believer.

Young Singles

Ages 18–24

Young adults stepping into independence, identity, and purpose. Building strong spiritual foundations for the journey ahead.

Due Singles

Ages 25–29

Singles in the season of expectation and preparation. Trusting God's timing while actively developing in every area of life.

Adult Singles

Ages 30+

Mature believers walking in faith, career, and purpose — pressing forward with confidence that God's plan is perfect.

Single Parents

All Ages

Parents navigating life solo, raising children in the fear of God. Supported, valued, and empowered for the next chapter.

What We Do

Programs & Activities

Gen218 offers a rich calendar of spiritual, educational, and social activities designed to build whole and ready believers.

Monthly Meetings

Regular gatherings with powerful teachings from the Word of God, testimonies, prayer sessions, and fellowship. Every meeting is an encounter with the God who answers by fire.

Seminars & Conferences

Focused teaching events covering relationships, career, finances, emotional health, spiritual warfare, and preparation for marriage — equipping singles on every plane.

Fellowship & Social Activities

Outings, retreats, game nights, and community events that build genuine friendships and create space for God to connect hearts in wholesome, faith-centered fellowship.
