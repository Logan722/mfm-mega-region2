About Us

A Do-It-Yourself Gospel Ministry

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a full gospel ministry where your hands are trained to wage war and your fingers to do battle. Founded by Dr. D.K. Olukoya, MFM is devoted to the revival of apostolic signs, wonders, and miracles.

Mega Region 2 USA, headquartered at MFM Prayer City in Houston, TX, is led by Pastor Olumide Oni — a prophet, evangelist, pastor, teacher, and prolific author of over 107 anointed books.

"Blessed be the Lord my strength, which teacheth my hands to war, and my fingers to fight." — Psalm 144:1
