Meet Our Pastors

Pastor Olumide & Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni

Principal Regional Overseer & Co-Laborer, Mega Region 2

Pastor Olumide Oni is a prophet, evangelist, pastor, teacher, husband and father. He currently serves as the Principal Regional Overseer of MFM Mega Region 2 in North America, headquartered at MFM USA Prayer City in Houston, Texas.

A prolific author of over 107 anointed books, Pastor Oni is known for dynamic teaching, administrative excellence, and a powerful personal testimony of God's deliverance.
