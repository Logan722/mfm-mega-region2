Ways to Give

Your Seed, His Harvest

Every offering is an act of faith and worship. Choose a giving category below.

Tithes

The firstfruit of your increase belongs to the Lord. Honouring God with your tithe opens the windows of heaven over your life.

Offerings

A freewill expression of gratitude and worship. Give generously as the Lord has blessed you, and watch Him multiply your seed.

Special Seeds

Targeted seeds of faith — sown in expectation of specific breakthroughs, miracles, and divine intervention in your situation.

Building Fund

Partner in building the house of God. Your contribution helps establish and expand MFM Prayer City and regional branches across America.

How to Give

Payment Options

We are preparing secure and convenient ways for you to give online, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment platforms. In the meantime, you can give your tithes and offerings during any of our services at Prayer City or your local MFM branch.

For questions about giving, please contact us at (832) 788-8504 or visit Prayer City HQ.
